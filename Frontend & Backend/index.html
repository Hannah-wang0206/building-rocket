SYNTHESIS SPACE PROGRAM

Welcome to our interactive page on aerospace science, a virtual platform designed for aerospace enthusiasts and professionals. Here you will have the opportunity to take on the role of an aerospace worker and experience the entire process from designing to launching a rocket. With our Stable Diffusion technology, you can turn your rocket design sketches into realistic images. Not only that, but you can input flight operation parameters such as fuel and initial velocity, and perform calculations using Orkovsky's rocket equations to simulate the rocket's trajectory and orbit. Our web page also provides orbit visualisation, allowing you to visualise the rocket's path through space. Now, let's begin this exciting journey into space!
